AI-Powered Microwave Circuit Design: 10x Faster, More Affordable, and Higher Quality

Our purpose

We use AI to improve microwave circuit design, making circuits faster, more affordable, and higher quality for the aerospace, defense, and telecommunications fields.

Circuit design is broken

Microwave Circuit Design Today is Slow, Expensive, and Constrained by Outdated Methods Engineers are stuck with manual, iterative processes that result in:

  • Prolonged development cycles - Delaying critical innovations
  • High costs – Increasing barriers to entry and scaling
  • Limited design flexibility – Limiting optimization and performance.
These inefficiencies hinder the deployment of next-gen technologies in defense, aerospace, and telecommunications, slowing industry growth and innovation. The market demands a faster, smarter, and more scalable solution.

Our Solution

We have developed an AI-powered software tool that leverages genetic optimization and inverse design to:

  • Automate & accelerate circuit design
  • Reduce production costs
  • Unlock innovative, high-performance designs
